Automated Aeroponic based irrigation system using Arduino.

System discription

The world is facing a number of serious problems of which population rise, climate change, soil degradation, water scarcity and food security are among the most important. Due rapid urbanization the agricultural lands are shrinking. In modern world agriculture as many forms like hydroponic agriculture, aeroponic agriculture and aquaponic agriculture . Aeroponics is a culture technique where the plant roots are suspended in the air and they are intermittently sprayed with a nutrient solution. Aeroponic has several advantages over traditional agriculture, like reduce the consumption of water and increased growth rate and plant density and also have disadvantages like it requires artificial nutrients for plant growth. In our system we are using natural nutrient rich aqua waste water as a nutrient solution. It is aimed to maximize nutrient availability at the root zone, thus helping to maximize plant growth, improve the efficiency and environment impact of agriculture. Automatic aeroponic-irrigation system is based on IoT and ArduinoUno. Sensors for measuring the temperature, light and pH of water. Meanwhile, the control system was used to manage sensor’s data and transmitted via GSM in order to facilitate easier monitoring for users. Solar energy is used as a power source.
